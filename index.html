About Me

I am an Assistant Professor (Grade-II) in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Dr B R Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT Jalandhar), Punjab. Prior to this, I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi.


I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from IIT (ISM) Dhanbad in 2019, and my B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Vinoba Bhave University (VBU), Hazaribagh, Jharkhand.


My research interests are in the area of Power Electronics, including Multilevel Inverters, Renewable Energy (Grid-Connected Systems), Active Power Factor Correction (PFC), and DC-DC Converters for Solar PV and Electric Vehicle applications.
