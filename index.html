About Me

I am a faculty member in the Department of Electrical Engineering, IITB, India. Before this, I was at IITK, during 2013-2020. I was also a visiting scholar at WSU, Pullman, US, and Queen's University, Kingston, CA during Summer 2018 and Winter 2018, respectively. During 2012-13, I was a postdoctoral research associate at WSU, Pullman, US.

I received Ph.D. and B.Tech degrees in EE from IITB. I have worked on solar PV systems, UPS systems, and digital controllers.
